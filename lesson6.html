Farming Facilities

include facilities ranging from entire holdings to installations such as irrigation systems, drainage systems, greenhouses, stables, barns.

Irrigation Facilities

The NIA-Pula Bansud River Irrigation System has been operating since 1960. Since then, several rehabilitation works were undertaken through the Irrigation Operation Support Project (IOSP). Irrigation water is diverted through a run-off type river dam located in Barangay Inclanay, across the Pula River.

Municipal Slaughterhouse

There is a municipal slaughterhouse in Pinamalayan. It is located at a one hectare lot in Barangay Papandayan. It has a capacity of 16 tons and makes use of Biogas Technology for waste treatment.

Agricultural Center & Breeding Stations

The establishment of Trichoderma Laboratory strengthen the campaign of this office to scatter rice straw after harvesting. The application of trichoderma hastens the decomposition process which is distributed by this office for free. Said laboratory produced two hundred (200) bottles of trichoderma which was used by the farmers.

Farm Equipment and Warehouses

The most common farm equipment used in the municipality consists of tractors rice threshers, rice mills and others. The municipality has a total of 32 rice mills, 66 warehouses, 4 mechanical dryers, 67 irrigation pump, 102 solar dryers, 213 rice threshers, 363 hand tractors and accessories and 1 cold storage with 72 tons capacity.
